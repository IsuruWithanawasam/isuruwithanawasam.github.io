The Narrative

I am a researcher bridging the gap between fundamental physics and medical application. Currently, I am a Master’s student in the Erasmus Mundus LASCALA program, studying across premier institutions in Hungary, Sweden, and France.

My work creates a synergy between Nanomaterials (my BSc background) and Applied Photonics. I am passionate about developing non-invasive optical instrumentation to solve biological challenges.
